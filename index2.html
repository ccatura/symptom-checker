Take Charge Of Your Diabetes

A six-week evidence-based program that helps you take day-to-day responsibility for your diabetes care. You will gain skills necessary to self-manage your diabetes and work effectively with your healthcare professionals.

Living Well With Chronic Conditions

A six-week evidence-based group education program that teaches you practical skills you need to live well with a variety of health conditions. Through weekly sessions, the workshop provides support for continuing normal daily activities and dealing with emotions that arise from having a chronic condition.

Live Healthy Work Healthy

LHWH is a 6-week workshop series that provides tools for living a healthy life in the workplace with chronic health conditions, including diabetes, arthritis, cancer, obesity, heart disease and long-term COVID.
